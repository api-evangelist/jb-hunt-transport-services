J.B. Hunt Transport Services was approved for a dual listing on the newly established Nasdaq Texas exchange, with trading beginning March 6, 2026 under the symbol "JBHT." The stock continues trading on the Nasdaq Global Select Market, giving the company expanded access to capital and investors while supporting growth in Texas and the southeastern United States.
