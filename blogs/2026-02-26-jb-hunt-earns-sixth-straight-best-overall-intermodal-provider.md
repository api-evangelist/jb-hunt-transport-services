J.B. Hunt earned best overall domestic intermodal provider recognition for the sixth consecutive time in the Journal of Commerce Intermodal Service Scorecard, achieving a 4.6 out of 5 overall score and a 93% customer satisfaction rating based on feedback from more than 145 shippers and intermodal companies surveyed between July and December 2025.
