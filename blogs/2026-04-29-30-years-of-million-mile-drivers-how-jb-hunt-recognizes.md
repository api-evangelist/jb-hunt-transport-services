J.B. Hunt honored 54 drivers on April 29, 2026 for reaching between 2 and 5 million safe miles without a DOT-reportable preventable accident, marking the 30th anniversary of its Million Mile program. The event, held at company headquarters in Lowell, Arkansas, recognized a program that has now honored more than 5,000 drivers who reached at least 1 million safe miles.
