J.B. Hunt reported first quarter 2026 revenue of $3.06 billion, up 5%, with operating income of $207.0 million, up 16%, and diluted earnings per share of $1.49, up 27% from $1.17 a year earlier. CEO Shelley Simpson credited operational excellence and noted the company set a first-quarter safety record despite winter weather and elevated demand.
