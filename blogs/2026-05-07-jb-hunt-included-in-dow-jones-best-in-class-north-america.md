J.B. Hunt Transport Services has been included in the Dow Jones Best-in-Class North America Index following strong performance in S&P Global's 2025 Corporate Sustainability Assessment. The company is the only trucking company among four ground transportation firms in the index and is targeting a 32% cut in carbon emissions intensity by 2034. Its intermodal service has helped avoid an estimated 30 million metric tons of CO2e emissions over the past decade.
